Forza Horizon 5

Details

Forza Horizon 5 was my first full project cycle in the industry. I joined during its concept phase and saw it through to ship and beyond including its first expansion - Forza Horizon 5 Hot Wheels.

During this time, I worked on an enormous variety of tools and workflows, including the authoring and build pipeline tooling for the procedural world generation system. Day-to-day I work closely with world-class artists, designers, and content creators to create, refine, improve, and redesign an immense variety of workflows. I enjoy listening to them carefully, watching them work, and collaboratively reaching the best solution.
